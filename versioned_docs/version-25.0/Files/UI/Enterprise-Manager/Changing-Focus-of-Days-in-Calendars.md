# Changing Focus of Days in Calendars

To change the focus of a day:

1. Double-click on **Matrix** under the **Operation** topic. The **Matrix** screen displays.
2. Click the **First** button to change the focus to the first day in the Daily tables.
3. Click the **Today** button to change the focus to the current day's date.
4. Click the **Last** button to change the focus to the last day for which there are currently schedules in the Daily tables.
5. Click on a **date** that has a green background.
6. Click on the **schedule** to view the schedule information in the Daily tables.
